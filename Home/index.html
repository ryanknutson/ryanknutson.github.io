How I Design

Follows Standards

I design my websites based on the latest design standards set by the W3C. This insures that your website will work on all newer browsers, with fixes for the old ones.

Responsive

Responsive designed websites are ones that are made to work on tablet, mobile, desktop, and any other device you may have. I make my websites this way for maximum compatibility and ease of use.

Clean

I use the Bootstrap Framework, by Twitter, to aid in the style my websites. This framework provides modern styling components, and tools to make the site look cleaner and newer.
