Bike Ride Trips

  1. Home
  2. Trips

Rules

  • Riders must be over 13 years of age
  • Riders must be fit enough to keep up with the group, if a rider is slowing the rest of the group down drastically then we will be forced to leave them behind
  • Attendance is free

11 Mile Bike Ride

December 6, 2014

On this gorgeous ride we will be leaving the bike shop as normal and riding to and around Central Park. This ride starts at 8:00 AM and is great for average bikers.

60 Mile Bike Ride

January 3, 2015

This bike ride is advised only to advanced riders with great stamina. In this ride we will be leaving the shop at 7:00 AM in the morning and will be stopping for lunch as a group in the afternoon. This ride is beautiful but requires serious cyclists. Mr. Zhivenkov will be leading this trip himself out of New York City and into some of the surrounding area!

5 Bike Ride

February 7, 2015

This ride is only 5 miles and so is suitable to novice cyclists. We will just be going in a small loop around the city beginning from the shop at 9:00 AM.
