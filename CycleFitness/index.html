Seminars

We offer several in-depth seminars that expound on choosing the right bike for you, bike safety, and how to cycle for fitness. We speak at community centers, schools, gyms, business meetings, and more. Schedule now for us to come speak at your next event, or look at our upcoming seminars that are open to the public.

Monthly Cycle Trip

Do you ever feel like cycling around New York City with a group? We sponsor a monthly cycle trip and make it available to the public. This is just one way that we give back to our wonderful community. Look at our Trips page to find out when the next trip is and come join us!

FAQ

Visit our FAQ to find the answers to some of the common questions that cyclists have. If your question is still not answered, feel free to contact our staff who will reply as quickly as possible. This is a great resource for beginning cyclists.
